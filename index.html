ORIGINS

The first records of cats in history are in Egypt.

There is a legend that states that these animals were sent by the gods to protect humanity from pests, as they would hunt rodents, thus preventing the spread of diseases.

So revered were they that there were laws in Ancient Egypt that punished anyone who harmed a cat or removed it from Egyptian territory.

THE PERFECT WAY OF WALKING

Cats step on their hind legs where their forelegs left their mark, a quality that earns them the nickname "perfect walkers."

A strategy in which many muscles of your body are involved, and that allows you to move forward with the least effort and as stealth as possible.
